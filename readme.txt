== Description ==

If you're looking for a way to display your product related videos directly from Youtube, look no further than Videos for WooCommerce. This plugin will help increase conversion rates by providing potential customers with a closer look at what you have to offer.

With Videos for WooCommerce, you can quickly and easily add product-related videos from Youtube to your WooCommerce store, increasing conversion rates and providing potential customers with more information about your products.

To make it happen, simply enter the YouTube API into the plugin's settings page, and a Videos tabs will be added to make a way showing your product videos from YouTube. The video will automatically be displayed on your product pages. More settings and control coming soon...

Videos are a great way to show off your products and this plugin allows you to easily embed videos from YouTube right into your product descriptions. Videos For WooCommerce is an excellent plugin used to increase conversion rates through showing product related videos on your eCommerce website. 

Using the plugin, you can easily embed your product related videos to your WooCommerce store and increase conversion rates. Videos will display as a beautiful grid on your product pages, and customers can watch them right from your site without ever having to leave.

With Videos for WooCommerce, you can quickly and easily add videos to your product pages – no programming required. Add a video tour of your product, or a demonstration of how it works. This plugin lets you easily embed videos from YouTube into your product pages. This can be a great way to show off your products in action.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How does it work? =

It searches youtube using your product title and displays the searched result videos as related product videos in a nicely designed grid.

= How much accuracy does it have? =

It completely depends on your product title. If your product title is too generic then you'll get generic videos.


== Installation ==

= Minimum Requirements =

* PHP 7.0 or greater is recommended
* WooCommerce 3.0 or greater is better
* WordPress 5.5 or greater is better

= Automatic installation =

Automatic installation is the easiest option -- WordPress will handle the file transfer, and you won’t need to leave your web browser. To do an automatic install of Youtube Videos For WooCommerce, log in to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to the Plugins menu, and click “Add New.”
 
In the search field type “Youtube Videos For WooCommerce,” then click “Search Plugins.” Once you’ve found us,  you can view details about it such as the point release, rating, and description. Most importantly of course, you can install it by! Click “Install Now,” and WordPress will take it from there.

= Manual installation =

Manual installation method requires downloading the Youtube Videos For WooCommerce plugin and uploading it to your web server via your favorite FTP application. The WordPress codex contains [instructions on how to do this here](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#manual-plugin-installation).
